About the Smart Last-Call Notification System

Our goal is to develop a smart last-call notification system that ensures passengers board their flights on time by providing automated call alerts. This innovative approach enhances the passenger experience and reduces the chances of missed flights.

Idea/Approach

The system is designed to keep passengers informed of critical flight information and boarding updates through various notification channels, such as SMS, calls, and emails.

Overview of the System

  • Flight Data Integration: Connect to real-time databases for schedules, boarding times, and gate updates.
  • Passenger Preferences: Gather contact details and preferences to send personalized alerts.
  • Automated Alerts: Trigger "last call" notifications through various methods.
  • Customizable Options: Allow passengers to customize alerts and adjust timing.
